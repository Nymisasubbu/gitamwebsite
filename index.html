About the University

GITAM (Deemed to be University) is a well-known institution located in Visakhapatnam. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various fields. The campus is close to the beach and is known for its peaceful environment and modern facilities.

Students enjoy an active learning atmosphere, supported by experienced faculty, advanced laboratories, and clubs that encourage overall personality development.
