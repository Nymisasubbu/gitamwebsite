Admissions

GITAM University offers admissions into various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Students can apply online through the official admission portal.

Eligibility

  • Minimum 60% in Intermediate or equivalent
  • Valid score in GAT / JEE / EAPCET (depending on the course)

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Passport-size Photos
  • Valid ID Proof
